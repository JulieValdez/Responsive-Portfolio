About Me

Hi. I am 45 years old and have a 21 year old son. He is a mechanical engineering major at UCSD. The picture you see to the right is my newest little monster Bill Murray. :) Technically he was not allowed to go in IKEA...but I am not the best rule follower. If you have never heard of Taft, don't worry, you aren't alone...but this tiny town is the epicenter of some of the largest oil reserves in the nation. Yes, nation! And was part of the Teapot Dome scandal in the 1920's.

When I am not at my regular job or pulling my hair out trying to learn how to do this homeowrk assignment, I teach group fitness. I teach a Les Mills format called BodyCombat. Google it, it's fun :)
